Lastly the needle Options

Needles are made from wood, plastic and metal. They also come in single straight, circular, and double points.

You can pick the needles that will work best for the project. The pattern will make suggestions on the size type.

You can use their suggestions or pick out your own needles. Once you have the needles, check your gauge before completing the project. Everyone knits to a different tension. You don't want your final project to end up too big or too small!
